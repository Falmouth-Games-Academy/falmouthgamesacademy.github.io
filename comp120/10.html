In this session, you will review the programs of two peers. You are expected to provide insight into the creative aspects of the work as well as evaluate the quality of the source code. This is an important opportunity to receive feedback on your programming practice and to improve your program prior to submission.

Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the assessment criteria for computer programs
  • Evaluatet a computer program to assess its quality in terms of given assessment criteria
Agenda:

0055 - 0140 Peer Review #1
0140 - 0200 Coffee Break
0200 - 0245 Peer Review #2
0245 - 0250 Debrief
0250 - 0300 Departure

Further Learning:
  • Read Chapters 10 and 11 of Martin (2008)
  • Review your programs and reflect on their quality in preparation for the programming viva in the next session
