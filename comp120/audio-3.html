This session introduces you to more complex tone generation that can be accomplished using additive techniques. Further to this, you will explore how to combine tones and envelopes to produce more interesting timbres as well as how to play tones in sequence to produce a melody. This is important because many sound effects in games are dynamic (e.g. engine noises affected by revs and fuel injection) and procedurally generated melodies can be leveraged creatively to better engage and immerse players.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain how a basic additive synthesizer works
  • Write a function to generate a musical note and manipulate its timbre
  • Write a program that can convert musical notation into a melody
