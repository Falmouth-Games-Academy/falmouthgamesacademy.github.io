This session introduces you to how sound is digitized. Audio is used extensively in games, and so a basic knowledge of how to manipulate and combine digital audio will enable you to create interesting and creative sound effects in your games.

Learning Objectives:
  • Recognise how audio is used in games
  • Explain what sound is and how it can be represented digitally
  • Write a program that will produce a sound
