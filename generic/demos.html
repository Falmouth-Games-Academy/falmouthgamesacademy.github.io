In this session you will---with your team---demonstrate your collaborative game projects. This is an exciting opportunity to show-off your progress, get some feedback from tutors and peers, as well as play-testing to improve the design and highlight bugs. Please use the latest stable build of the game project.

Students enrolled on COMP130, COMP230, COMP240, COMP330, and COMP340 will also showcase posters that describe their individual contributions and/or innovate technology that they have incorporated into their projects.

However, it is also an opportunity to see what other developers in the Games Academy are working on. As such, you are required to attend for the entire day. Throughout the day, you will either be playing or presenting!

The day is arranged into three parts, each corresponding to modules at particular stages of the degree ---first-year students in the morning, second-year students in the afternoon, and final-year students in the early evening. Different members of staff will come to different sessions to review all of the games for a particular module. The team must be present at these review as feedback will be provided in person.
