In this session you will be introduced to the Turing machine, an important mathematical abstraction of a general-purpose computer.

You will also be introduced to the resources, both physical and electronic, available to you through the university library. These will be invaluable to you in writing your research journals and carrying out independent research.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the importance and operation of a Turing machine
  • Use the resources available in the university library
  • Apply computational thinking to puzzle solving
