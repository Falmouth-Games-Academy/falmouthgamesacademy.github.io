This session introduces the computer: its history, its architecture and the languages that can be used to program it. This lays the foundation for your subsequent studies of computing.

Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the historical context of computing and gaming technology
  • Explain the basic architecture of a computer
  • Distinguish the most common programming languages and paradigms in use today
