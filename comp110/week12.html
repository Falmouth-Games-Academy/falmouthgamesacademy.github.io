Computers were built by mathematicians, and computing professionals cannot avoid knowing and applying some mathematics. In this session you will also have the opportunity to raise any questions or concerns regarding mathematics.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain why mathematics is important in computing
  • Recall key concepts from arithmetic, algebra and geometry
