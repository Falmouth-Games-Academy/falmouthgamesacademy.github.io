Alongside your new peers, you will design and build a LEGO EV3 robot and write a program that will command the robot on a simulated space mission. These induction tasks are designed to welcome you to our community and to help develop your interpersonal skills.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of these sessions, you should be able to:

  • Recognise the value of collaboration in software development
  • Describe at least ONE interesting fact about both yourself and your immediate peers
  • Apply basic engineering and computational thinking skills to solve a set of problems
  • Constructively analyse the contribution of at least TWO peers
