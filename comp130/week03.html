This session builds upon your knowledge of object-orientated programming (OOP), introducing some advanced OOP design principles. OOP is the dominant paradigm in software development today, and in-depth understanding of it is vital to writing maintainable and efficient programs.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain advanced principles of object-oriented design
  • Write C++ programs putting these principles into practice
