This is a guest lecture by Simon Colton. Professor Colton leads the MetaMakers Institute research group at Falmouth University, and is a world-renowned expert in the field of computational creativity: the design of artificial intelligence systems that can perform tasks which one generally thinks of as requiring creativity, such as the creation of art.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain the key concepts and successes of computational creativity
