This session introduces basic algorithms for Artificial Intelligence and Procedural Content Generation. These topics will be covered in more detail in your second year; this session serves as a taster and to let you begin exploring these techniques.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the operation of basic AI algorithms such as A* pathfinding, Growing Tree maze generation, and Finite State Machine agent behaviours
  • Implement these techniques in your own programs
