This is a joint session with BA Games Development.

What is a post-mortem and why do we need them? In this lecture, we will understand why conducting a post-mortem will benefit the team on future projects. Conducting a post-mortem is not a tick box activity, but something that is a working document that you need to act on. With examples, we will identify the process for conducting a good constructive review that will enable the team to reflect on the lessons learned and specify new guidelines to follow in the future.
