In this session you will be introduced to the Sony Playstation 4 development kit.

For legal reasons, you must attend this induction and sign a declaration in order to gain access to the PS4 development kits. This is your only opportunity for an induction. If you do not attend, you will be barred from accessing the PS4 dev kits and will be restricted to mobile platforms for the COMP350 module in the next study block.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Operate the PS4 development kit hardware
  • Explain the legal restrictions on your use of the PS4 development kits and associated software
