This session will introduce the MIPS CPU architecture and its associated assembly language. The MIPS architecture was used in several games consoles, including the Nintendo 64 and the Sony Playstation 2. As such it is an ideal architecture to study as a bridge between the 6502 and modern architectures such as Intel x86.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain the historical significance of the MIPS CPU architecture
  • Identify the key components of the MIPS architecture
  • Read and write simple programs in MIPS assembly language
