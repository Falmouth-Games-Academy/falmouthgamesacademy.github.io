This session will introduce the concept of a de-make, and help you begin to formulate your proposal for this module's main portfolio project. De-makes are a valuable exercise for game designers to reflect on the core components of a design, whilst staying within the constraints of limited hardware.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain the main technical constraints of the NES architecture
  • Account for these constraints in the context of a game design
