This session will introduce the 6502 CPU architecture and its associated assembly language. The aim of this module is to give you a more intimate understanding of the low-level operation of a computer architecture. Understanding and programming architectures from the 1980s is much simpler than fully understanding the complex architectures in use today, but still illustrates the underlying principles.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain the historical significance of the 6502 CPU
  • Identify the key components of the 6502 architecture
  • Read and write simple programs in 6502 assembly language
