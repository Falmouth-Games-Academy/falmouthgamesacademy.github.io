We will begin this session by exploring human-machine symbiosis through human factors, perception, and VR/AR technology. We will discuss all of these subjects under the concept of immersion. Immersion is the end goal of all VR/AR development.

The second part of this session will focus on the practical aspects of creating AR/VR systems. You will gain first-hand experience working with a variety of different AR/VR systems. There will be time towards the end of the session for continual development of the assignment task COMP210_2 where you will have the opportunity to get support and feedback on your process so far.


Learning Objectives: Immersion LOs:
  • Discuss the term immersion relative to AR/VR system design
  • Explain the importance of human-factors in relation to human-centered design
  • List and describe the various perceptual modalities
Practical Activity LOs:
  • Demonstrate a technical proficiency using various AR/VR systems
  • Discuss the strengths, weaknesses, and limitations of various AR/VR systems
  • Consider how best to integrate the appropriate hardware into the assignment task
