This week we will focus on planning and carrying out your research. We will start by discussing the difference between qualitative and quantitative research and then explore the different methods of generating both qualitative and quantitative data. There will be some time towards the end of the session for you to plan your research.


Learning Objectives:
  • Outline the importance of quantitative and qualitative research
  • List The various methods of collecting data relative to user-research in games
  • Plan future research activities
