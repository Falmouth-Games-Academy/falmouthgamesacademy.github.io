The focus for this week will be quantitative research methods, analysis and reporting. We will start with a introductory presentation that outlines the importance of this type of research and then discuss the various methods of quantitative research. We will then discuss the best practices for analysing the data and reporting your findings.


Learning Objectives:

  • Explain the importance of human-factors in relation to human-centered design
  • List and describe the various perceptual modalities
