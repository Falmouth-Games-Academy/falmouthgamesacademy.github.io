This session explores human-machine symbiosis through human perception, ergonomics, and VR/AR technology.


Learning Objectives:
  • Discuss the term immersion relative to AR/VR system design
  • Explain the importance of ergonomics in relation to human-centered design
  • List and describe the various perceptual modalities
