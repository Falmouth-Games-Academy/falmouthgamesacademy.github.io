This week we will focus on quantitative research methods, analysis and reporting. We will start with a introductory presentation that outlines the importance of this type of research and then discuss the various methods of quantitative research. We will then review approaches to analysing the data and best practice for reporting your findings.


Learning Objectives:
  • Outline the importance of quantitative research
  • List The various methods of collecting quantitative data relative to user-research in games
  • List the sections of a report and explain their content
