This week we dissect the term 'immersion'. What factors coalesce in order to immerse the user in an experience?

Lecture:

Teleporting the user into new worlds can be an exciting prospect but it is vital to remember that we are dealing with real people. We must ensure that we keep the user at the center of the design process and ensure their comfort and safety. In order to do this, we must consider human factors when working with immersive systems. This week we will explore the human factors of VR though the term immersion and begin to create a set of guiding principles for good UX.

In addition to the normal lecture, there is an additional afternoon session where you will be expected to present your assignment proposals.

Learning Objectives:
  • Discuss the term immersion relative to AR/VR system design
  • Explain the importance of human-factors in relation to human-centered design
  • List and describe the various perceptual modalities
