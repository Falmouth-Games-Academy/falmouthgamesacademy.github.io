This week will focus on usability methods that generate qualitative data. Starting with a general overview the discussion will narrow to look more indepth at one particular method known as heuristic evaluation. We will carry out a class activity where we analyse a game UI based on a specific heuristic set and share the results. This will inform your approach to assignment 1.


Learning Objectives:
  • Outline the importance of qualitative research
  • List The various HCI research methods of collecting qualitative data
  • Plan future heuristic evaluations in relation to assignment 1
