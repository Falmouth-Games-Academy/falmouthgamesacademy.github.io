This session explores human-machine symbiosis through human factors, perception, and VR/AR technology. We will discuss all of these subjects under the concept of immersion. Immersion is the end goal of all VR/AR development.


Learning Objectives:
  • Discuss the term immersion relative to AR/VR system design
  • Explain the importance of human-factors in relation to human-centered design
  • List and describe the various perceptual modalities
