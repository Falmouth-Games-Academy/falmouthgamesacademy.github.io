Lecture:

This week discuss HCI research methods with a particular focus on the distinction between qualitative and quantitative data. Starting with a general overview the discussion will narrow to the methods relevant to the practical aspects of the module assignments.

Workshop:

The workshop this week, will provide instruction on how to plan the evaluation required by the practical assignment. You will learn how to formulate questions that elicit meaningful responses.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the difference between qualitative and quantitative data
  • List The various HCI research methods
  • Choose the right research methods given a specific hypothesis
