This week we will focus on the analysis and reporting of your results. We will begin by discussing techniques for codifying qualitative data and then move on to the analysis and visualisation of quantitative data. Once we have fully explored the analysis process we will then discuss approaches to reporting your results.


Learning Objectives:
  • Extrapolate trends, patterns and interesting features from qualitative data using coding techniques
  • Analyse and visualise quantitative data
  • Report findings in a structured and appropriate manner
