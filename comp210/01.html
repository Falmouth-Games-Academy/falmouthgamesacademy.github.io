The first half of this session introduces some basic concepts for constructing virtual and augmented reality systems. The second half outlines the core principles of Human-Centred Design and explores methods of qualitative and quantitative research that put the user at the heart of the design process.


Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the importance of placing the user at the center of the design process
  • Briefly describe and compare different user-centered design research methods
  • Demonstrate a knowledge of the core principles of user-centered design
  • List and describe the key components that make up the hardware side of reality systems.
