This session focuses on the practical aspects of creating AR/VR systems. You will gain first-hand experience working with a variety of different AR/VR systems. There will be time towards the end of the session for continual development of the assignment task COMP210_2 where you will have the opportunity to get support and feedback on your process so far.


Learning Objectives:
  • Demonstrate a technical proficiency using various AR/VR systems
  • Discuss the strengths, weaknesses, and limitations of various AR/VR systems
  • Consider how best to integrate the appropriate hardware into the assignment task
