Lecture:

The process of designing, planning and carrying out data collection is only half the battle. Once all the data is in, it needs to validated, cleaned, transformed, visualised and interpreted. In this session we will cover some basic techniques for validating, visualising and interpreting data.

Workshop

The workshop this week, you will experience play testing first hand. You will put the Augmented Telegrapher prototype through it's paces and complete a survey at the end. Hopefully, by the end of the workshop you will understand the kind of planning that is involved in implementing play testing.

Learning Objectives:
  • Validate and clean data
  • Visualise data using various graphic representations
  • Interpret data using descriptive statistics
