In this final dialogue, you will explore how to influence and maintain the culture of the games industry. Namely, in the context of: professional bodies; continuing professional development; and activism. These are important issues because they will feed into how you will conduct yourself as a computing professional in the future.

Please also note, you are expected to engage in independent research on these topics prior to the session in order to effectively engage in the dialogue.


Morning Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Analyse the role of professional bodies to promote and maintain ethical working practices
  • Analyse the role of continuing professional development beyond self-enrichment (e.g., worldly-enrichment)
  • Evaluate the role of activism and outreach in promoting socio-cultural change
