This week we will assume the position of expert and interrogate user-interface (UI) against a set of heuristic. The Heuristic Evaluation, a process made famous by Nielsen, is a type of qualitative research that draws upon the experience and knowledge of experts to identify UI issues early on in the design stage. We will begin by discussing the methodology of a heuristic evaluation and then in the second part of the session, we will carry out a Heuristic Evaluation using the original heuristics outlined by Nielsen.


Learning Objectives:
  • Explain what is meant by the term Heuristic Evaluation
  • Justify the choice of heuristics given the application
  • Implement a Heuristic Evaluation on any UI
