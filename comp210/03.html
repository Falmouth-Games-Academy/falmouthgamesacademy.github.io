This session explores the various psychological considerations that are vital to AR/VR system design and more specifically the factors that are crucial to the illusion of presence for the user.


Learning Objectives:
  • Discuss the term 'presence' relative to AR/VR system design
  • Explain the importance of psychology in relation to human-centered design and AR/VR
  • Discuss the difference between objective and subjective reality.
Agenda:
0000 - 0005 Introduction
0005 - 0050 Lecture - Presence
0050 - 0130 Activity - Object vs. Subjective reality
0130 - 0200 Coffee Break
0200 - 0250 Lecture - Processing information
0250 - 0300 Debrief


Further Learning:
