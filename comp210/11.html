In this session, you will review the AR/VR game interfaces of your peers. You are expected to provide insight into the creative aspects of the work as well as evaluate the quality of the source code. This is an important opportunity to receive feedback on your programming practice and to improve your interface prior to submission.


Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the assessment criteria for computer programs
  • Evaluate a computer program to assess its quality in terms of given assessment criteria
