This session covers how to conduct a literature review and formulate a research question. A strong research question, informed by a thorough review of relevant scholarly literature, is an essential first step in conducting a research project

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain what makes a good research question
  • Formulate research questions in the area of your chosen project
  • Conduct a scholarly literature review
