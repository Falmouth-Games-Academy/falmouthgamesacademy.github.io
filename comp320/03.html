This session covers the philosophical underpinnings of research as understood in terms of ontological, epistemological, axiological, and inferential positions. A clear, detailed, and justified methodology is critical to the integrity of the claims made in the dissertation, and so this forms an essential foundation to the research project write-up and the dissemination of your work.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Discuss the main characteristics of different research philosophies
  • Recognize the challenges associated with different forms of reasoning
  • Analyse how research methods are justified through underpinning philosophy
