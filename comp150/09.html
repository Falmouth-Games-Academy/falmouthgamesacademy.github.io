This session introduces more advanced techniques in software project management and helps you to explore the application of agile development practices in a practical way. Using branching, for example, is a key approach to distributing work to peers to be completed without causing disruption to the master version of the software. Such knowledge is essential in professional contexts and will demand considerable effort on your part to fully develop. As such, we introduce these techniques early to provide you sufficient time to practice across the degree course.
