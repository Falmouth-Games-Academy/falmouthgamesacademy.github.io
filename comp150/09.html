In this session, you will learn the role and importance of reflection and how it relates to your professional development. You are expected to provide insight into your learning experience and the quality of the game development practice that you have engaged with thus far . This is an important opportunity to receive feedback on your development practice as well as your research-in-progress and academic writing skill.


Workshop

  • Analyse the relevance and importance of the assessment criteria
  • Evaluate a scholarly paper to assess its quality in terms of given assessment criteria
