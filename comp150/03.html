This session is centred on communicating your game designs. You will conduct a 3-minute 'elevator pitch' to sell your own personal game concept to your peers. Ensure that you bring with you enough printed handouts for your peers. After which, a selection process based on multiple-transferable vote will be used to select the game designs to take forward and to allocate you to a team. You will be charged, in new teams, with developing a pre-production prototype of the chosen game.
