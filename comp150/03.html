This session introduces the agile philosophy as well as practical guidelines surrounding game development practice as they apply to software. This is important because you are studying digital games and many of the key principles and processes are drawn from the software design and software engineering literature. Once you develop your programming ability, you will need to apply the agile philosophy in order to efficiently create digital games.

  • Design a digital game
  • Recall the key components of a game design document
