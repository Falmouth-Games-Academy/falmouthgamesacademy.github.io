This session introduces key aspects of academic writing and contrasts it against non-academic writing. You will be expected to do research as part of this degree program, so a firm understanding of academic writing is essential. The best way to familiarize yourself with this style is to use it yourself. Communication skills are important because your job as a game designer is to convey ideas and concepts while your job as a game programmer is to collaboratively construct algorithms and architectures as well as share solutions to problems. Writing helps develop such abilities.
