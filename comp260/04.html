This session is the third of three to explore concurrency in depth. You will explore issues associated with liveness, performance, and testing. This leads a discussion on scalability and deployment. This will provide even further insight into the design of the architecture for the server for the educational MUD.


Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the concepts of liveness, performance, and testing
  • Analyse the quality of concurrent code
