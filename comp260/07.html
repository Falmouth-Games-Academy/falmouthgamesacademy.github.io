This session focuses on the principles of computer networking and client-server interaction. This serves as an introduction to implementing multiple clients to interact with the educational MUD. Each client will be different, and will serve a different purpose, for different players.


Learning Objectives:
  • Demonstrate a technical proficiency using various AR/VR systems
  • Discuss the strengths, weaknesses, and limitations of various AR/VR systems
  • Consider how best to integrate the appropriate hardware into the assignment task
Agenda:
0000 - 0005 Introduction
0005 - 0130 Dialogue - Platforms and Portability
0005 - 0130 Workshop - Sockets and Client-Server Interaction
0130 - 0200 Coffee Break
0205 - 0250 Workshop - Networking Principles and the OSI 7-layer Model
0250 - 0300 Debrief

Essential Practice:
  • Read Chapters II and III of Multiplayer Game Programming
  • Incorporate networking, serialisation, and synchronisation features into your MUD client as appropriate
