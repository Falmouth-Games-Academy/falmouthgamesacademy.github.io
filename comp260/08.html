This session is the first of three to explore computer networking in depth. You will explore issues associated with sockets, serialisation, replication, and network protocols. This will provide a foundation for further work developing your clients for the educational MUD.


Learning Objectives:
  • Design protocols for a multiplayer game
  • Compare TCP and UDP
  • Illustrate how to coordinate a game between client and server
Agenda:
0000 - 0005 Introduction
0005 - 0130 Workshop - Practical Client Development
0130 - 0200 Coffee Break
0200 - 0250 Dialogue - Network Protocols
0250 - 0300 Debrief

Essential Practice:
  • Read Chapters IV and V Multiplayer Game Programming
  • Incorporate networking, serialisation, and synchronisation features into your MUD client as appropriate
