In this session, you will review the MUD server and journal-in-progress that you have produced together and reflect on the key challenges that you encountered. You are expected to provide insight into the architectural quality of the distributed system as well as evaluate the quality of the source code. This is an important opportunity to receive feedback on your programming practice and to improve the MUD server (and critically its API, which you will be using to implement your clients) prior to submission.


Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the assessment criteria for computer programs and research journals
  • Evaluate a computer program to assess its quality
  • Evaluate a research journal to assess its quality

0135 - 0205 Coffee Break
