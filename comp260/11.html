In this session, you will review the MUD clients that you have created. You are expected to provide insight into the architectural quality of the distributed system as well as evaluate the quality of the source code. This is an important opportunity to receive feedback on your programming practice and to improve your MUD client prior to submission.


Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the assessment criteria for computer programs and research journals
  • Evaluate a computer program to assess its quality
  • Evaluate a research journal to assess its quality
