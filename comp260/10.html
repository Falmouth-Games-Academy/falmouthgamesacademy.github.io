This session is the third of three to explore computer networking in depth. You will explore issues associated with multiplayer security, paying particular attention to encryption, authentication, and avoiding common attacks such as man-in-the-middle. This will provide even further insight into the design of the architecture of your client for the educational MUD.


Learning Objectives:
  • Illustrate how to make a multiplayer game resilient against security concerns
  • Analyse the robustness of a multiplayer game against common forms of attack such as interception-in-the-middle
  • Explain how encryption and public-key infrastructure operate
