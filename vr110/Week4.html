VR110 Week 4: Embodiment

This week we will investigate the term 'embodiment' in relation to VR.

Lecture

Drawing upon some early research by Jaron Lanier and recent advances in interative avatars by the likes of FB, this week focuses on the implications of inhabiting VR spaces either in isolation, with AI or in social contexts.

Workshop

In the session we will be delving further into the oculus SDK tools, exploring Avatar interactions and social spaces.

  • Unity
  • Oculus Avatar SDK
Learning Objectives:

By the end of this week you should be able to:

  • Discuss the key theories surrounding embodied immersive experience
  • Experiment with embodiment within the immersive experience
  • Implement functionality from the Oculus Avatar SDK
