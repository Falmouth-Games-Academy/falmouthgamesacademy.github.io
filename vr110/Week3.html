VR110 Week 3: Immersion vs. Presence

This week we compare and contrast the terms immersion and presence to help us better understand the requirements of immersive systems.

Lecture

Teleporting the user into new worlds can be an exciting prospect but it is vital to remember that we are dealing with real people. We must ensure that we keep the user at the center of the design process and ensure their comfort and safety. In order to do this, we must consider human factors when working with immersive systems. This week we will explore the human factors of VR though the terms, immersion and presence and begin to create a set of guiding principes for good UX.

Workshop

This week's workshop will cover techniques for allowing users to navigate VR environments.

  • Unity
  • UX
Learning Objectives:

By the end of this week you should be able to:

  • Articulate the difference between the terms immersion and presence
  • Create immersive experiences that consider human factors
  • Create Unity environments and allow users to navigate through space.
