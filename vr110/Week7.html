VR110 Week 7: Story Telling in VR

This week we will discuss the importance of narrative in relation to immersion in VR worlds.

Lecture

Whether you are simulating realworld scnearios of creating absurd worlds of fun and chaos, narrative is key to engaging users. This week we will look at various theories around narrative design including the term suspension of disbelief, non-linear narrative and story arcs.

Workshop

This week's workshop will cover techniques for communicating with the user when they are inside the VR experience through UI that adheres to UX best practice.

  • Unity
  • UI Design
Learning Objectives:

By the end of this week you should be able to:

  • Define the term suspension of disbelief
  • Create user flows through Unity scenes
  • Integrate Fungi into environment for narrative structure
