VR110 Week 8: Traditional Sound Design

This week we will focus on the often overlooked art of sound design for VR.

Lecture

As a consequence of surviving thousands of years of evolution, humans have become extremely adept at localizing and identifying sounds in their environment. This makes sounds design a crucial consideration for any immersive experience. This week we will begin to explore theory, tools and techniques to support sound design for immersive experiences.

Workshop

This week's workshop will cover traditional sound design tools and techniques.

  • ZOOM Recorders
  • Adobe Auditions
  • Unity
Learning Objectives:

By the end of this week you should be able to:

  • Recall the main conponents of sound design
  • Capture sound using ZOOM recorders
  • Place sounds in Unity and control playback
