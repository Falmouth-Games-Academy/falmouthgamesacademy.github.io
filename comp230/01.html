You will review the course structure and its assignments, focusing on the first semester. You will also plan your time and showcase any work conducted during the summer period. These induction tasks are designed to prepare you for the forthcoming year and to enable you the opportunity to catch-up with your colleagues.


Workshop Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the structure of the course and the assignments
  • Reflect upon your personal and professional development over the Summer break
  • Explain the goal of your pre-production project
  • Analyse ideologies in ethics and relate them to computing professionals

Supervised Studio Practice

Your supervised studio hours are reserved for self-directed programming practice and contributions to your game development projects. A mentor will be available to facilitate these tasks.
