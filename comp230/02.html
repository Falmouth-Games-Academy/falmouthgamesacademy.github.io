This session introduces ethics and professionalism in the computing context, framing them as topics that can be explored through Bohmian dialogues. An awareness of ethical and professional issues in the games industry is important because they will influence your future working practice and job satisfaction. You also will need to appreciate the extra level of intellectual freedom that is expected of you, and the format of dialogues will help you to develop this independence.


Workshop Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the roles that computer systems have played in disasters and the implications these have for computing professionals
  • Differentiate between the key aspects of: discussions; debates; and dialogues
