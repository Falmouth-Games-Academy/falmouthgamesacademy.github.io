In this first dialogue, you will explore several controversies associated with digital games. Namely: representation; aggressive behavior; and addiction. These are important issues because they will feed into how you design games in the future.

Please also note, you are expected to engage in independent research on these topics prior to the session in order to effectively engage in the dialogue.
