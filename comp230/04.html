In this second dialogue, you will explore several controversies associated with work in the games industry. Namely, professional responsibilities in the following contexts: workplace relations; corporate relations; and customer relations. These are important issues to consider because they will help you engage with professional issues in your future working life.

Please also note, you are expected to engage in independent research on these topics prior to the session in order to effectively engage in the dialogue.


Workshop Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Analyse professional responsibilities in the workplace
  • Analyse professional responsibilities in the employee-employer relationship
  • Analyse professional responsibilities in the business-customer relationship
