In this session, you will review the research papers of two peers. You are expected to provide critical insight into the research topic and to critically evaluate both the quality of the scholarship and the quality of the academic writing. This is an important opportunity to receive feedback on your scholarly practice as well as your research-in-progress and academic writing skill.


Workshop Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the assessment criteria for scholarly papers
  • Analyse the relevance and importance of the assessment criteria
  • Evaluate a scholarly paper to assess its quality in terms of given assessment criteria

Supervised Studio Practice

Your supervised studio hours are reserved for self-directed programming practice and work on your coursework projects. A mentor will be available to facilitate these tasks.
