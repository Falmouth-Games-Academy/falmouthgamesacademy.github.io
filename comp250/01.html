This session will introduce the concept of artificial intelligence (AI), both in general and in its applications to games. Understanding the breadth of AI applications will help you identify opportunities to use it in your own games.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Define artificial intelligence (AI)
  • Explain how AI is used in modern video games
  • Identify ways to apply AI techniques in your own game development projects
