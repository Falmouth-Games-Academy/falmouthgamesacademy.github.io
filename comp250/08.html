Beyond the games industry, AI has the power to radically alter the way we live our lives. However this comes with ethical concerns: Will AI take people's jobs? Is our personal data safe? How can AI be held accountable for its decisions? Should we regulate the use of AI in applications such as healthcare and the military? This session will explore these issues and more.


Learning Objectives:
  • Explain how AI technology is transforming modern life
  • Debate the ethical issues surrounding the increased adoption of AI
