This session introduces the notion of search-based AI, looking at how heuristics can be used to apply it to more complex games. Heuristics are a way of injecting the designer's knowledge into AI systems, and are commonly used to improve efficiency and quality of decisions. We will also look at how similar techniques can be applied to planning problems in domains other than board games.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain the importance of heuristics in search-based AI
  • Design heuristics for search-based AI
  • Apply search-based AI to planning problems
