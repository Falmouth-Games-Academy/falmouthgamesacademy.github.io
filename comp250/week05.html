In this session, researchers from the MetaMakers Institute at Falmouth University will present their cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence and procedural content generation for games.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain recent advances in AI and PCG for games
  • Identify potential research topics in these areas for your final-year project
