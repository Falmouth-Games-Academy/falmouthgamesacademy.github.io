In this session you are expected to bring a final draft of your dissertation and will conduct a peer review. This is a valuable opportunity to receive feedback on your research findings. Alongside feedback and suggestions to improve the dissertation, each of your peers will be asked to recommend whether to: accept the dissertation; accept the dissertation subject to minor revisions; accept the dissertation subject to major revisions; or to reject the dissertation. It will be up to authors to ensure that the claims they are making are relevant and well-justified.
