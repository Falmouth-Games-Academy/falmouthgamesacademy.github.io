This session will introduce various ways of sanitising, interpreting and presenting the results of your statistical data analyses. These will build upon your knowledge of R, and increase your confidence with statistical computation. Statistical analysis of empirical data is an essential part of almost every empirical research project in computing, and so knowledge of the appropriate tools and techniques is vital to the effective communication and dissemination of your research findings.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Discuss how to present data and findings in academic papers
  • Use R to enhance data visualizations
