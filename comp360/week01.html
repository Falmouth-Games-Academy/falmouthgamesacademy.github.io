This session recaps the requirements for the final-year research dissertation. This final year project is your opportunity to undertake a significant research project, exploring an area of computing for games in depth and advancing the state of knowledge in the field.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Anticipate the content and challenges associated with writing the research dissertation
  • Discuss what will be expected of you during your final weeks on the undergraduate programme
  • Recall how to use the available online resources to learn more about the course
  • Recognise what research 'is' and the characteristics of high quality research
