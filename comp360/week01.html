This session recaps the requirements for the final-year research dissertation. This final year project is your opportunity to undertake a significant research project, exploring an area of computing for games in depth and advancing the state of knowledge in the field.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain the aims and expectations of the final deliverable: the dissertation
  • Apply appropriate methodologies to conduct scholarly research
  • Recognize the role and constraints that Falmouth University's policy on research ethics will have on your research conduct
  • Analyse the expectations and requirements set by professional bodies in the area of computing
