This session covers the underpinning characteristics and qualities of the written research dissertation, outlining how your work will be assessed and why such an approach is taken. This informs a review of two exemplar dissertations, which each possess strengths and weaknesses. This helps to form your ability to analyse research quality and therefore assess the quality of your own work and your peers' work.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Discuss the main characteristics of the written dissertation
  • Analyse the ways in which the main characteristics of the written dissertation can vary
  • Assess the qualities of a given dissertation
