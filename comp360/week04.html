This session will introduce various ways of interpreting and presenting the results of your statistical analyses. These will build upon your knowledge of R, a free software package for statistical computing. Statistical analysis of empirical data is an essential part of almost every research project, and so knowledge of the appropriate tools and techniques is vital to disseminating your research findings.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain how to present data and findings in academic papers
  • Use R to produce data visualizations
