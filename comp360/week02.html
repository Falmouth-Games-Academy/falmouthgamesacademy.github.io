This session covers how to formalize the management of the software development and data collection process. A clear, detailed, and justified methodology is critical to the integrity of the claims made in the dissertation, and so this forms an essential foundation to the research project write-up and the dissemination of your work.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Explain what makes a good project management methodology
  • Analyse your approach and schedule for the final-year project
  • Apply the use of a Gantt chart
  • Justify the use of research methods
