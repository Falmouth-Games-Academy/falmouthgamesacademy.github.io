The games industry exists to make money. Within its context, computing professionals have many opportunities to be entrepreneural, whether as inventors, consultants, or technical specialists holding a stake in their business. However, business is a very different skillset and does not come easily to everyone. This session introduces the business canvas, a way of conceiving the key elements of a business model. It is important for everyone to develop their commercial awareness, but it is also important feel empowered to setup your own studio in the future should you choose to do so.


Morning Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Explain what a business model is
  • Explain how a canvas can facilitate the construction of a sound business model
  • Analyse a case study to identify its business model
  • Propose a sound business model using a canvas-driven approach
