Digital games and digital game technology do not exist in a vacuum. They are, for the most part, commercial products and/or services. The game developers need to fund their craft, and make money. This requires a level of commercial awareness when embarking on game development projects. As such, this session emphasises the importance of evaluating a market and striving to develop a sound business case. It will also introduce the key deliverables of the production project.


Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the role of business, markets, products and services within a commercial context
