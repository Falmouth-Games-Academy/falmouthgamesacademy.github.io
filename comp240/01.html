Digital games and digital game technology do not exist in a vacuum. They are, for the most part, commercial artefacts. The game developers need to fund their craft, and make money. This requires a level of commercial awareness when embarking on game development projects. As such, this session emphasises the importance of evaluating a market and striving to develop a sound business case. It will also introduce the key deliverables of the production project.


Morning Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Explain why market evaluation is important
  • Explain how to conduct a market evaluation
  • Analyse a business case for a game development project
  • Propose a sound business case for a game
