Research is fundamental to a science degree. Computing professionals are not merely consumers of knowledge, but also producers of knowledge. Beyond research in technology, which you will study in depth next year, such professionals also conduct research in the commercial and humanistic domains. Today's session focuses on human research, drawing a distinction between different research designs, with a focus on quantitative and qualitative measurement. This is important as it will help you inform the market evaluation and business case for your written project.


Morning Session
Learning Objectives:
  • Compare research methods
  • Distinguish between qualitative and quantitative measurement
  • Assess the suitability of a research design for a given question
  • Design a suitable market research task
