Research is fundamental to a science degree. Computing professionals are not merely consumers of knowledge, but also producers of knowledge. Beyond research in technology, which you will study in depth next year, such professionals also conduct research in the commercial and humanistic domains. Today's session focuses on commercial research, drawing a distinction between primary and secondary approaches. This is important as it will help you inform the market evaluation and business case for your written project.
Morning Session
Learning Objectives:

  • Distinguish between primary and secondary research
  • Explain why businesses conduct primary research in the market research context
  • Outline the importance of research ethics
  • Assess the quality of secondary sources of market data and information
