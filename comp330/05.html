This session will examine the role of technical posters to improve technical interviews. We will look at the components of a 'good' poster and see how they relate to the skills required to excel in interviews

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Understand the differences between good, bad and indifferent technical posters
  • Use the technical poster model to create better posters
  • Appreciate the relationship between technical posters and technical interviews
