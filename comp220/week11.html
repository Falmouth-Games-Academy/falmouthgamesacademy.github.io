This session introduces collision detection and response. These are important for producing realistic physics simulations, but have applications even in games which do not use a physics engine, such as ensuring that the player cannot walk through walls.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the role of collision detection in 3D games
  • Recall basic techniques for collision detection
  • Write programs in which the player cannot walk through walls
