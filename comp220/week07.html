This session introduces the concepts of rigging and skeletal animation. These techniques are often used in modern games to produce animated characters.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the role of rigging in 3D animation
  • Describe how a rigged model is transformed to produce animation
  • Use skeletal animation in your own programs
