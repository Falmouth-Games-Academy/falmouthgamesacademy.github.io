This session introduces shader programs in OpenGL. Shaders are small programs which allow the parts of the graphics pipeline to be reprogrammed. Many advanced graphical effects rely on this.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the role of shaders in graphics programming
  • Distinguish the roles of the vertex shader and the fragment shader
  • Write simple shader programs in GLSL

0010 - 0040 Lecture: geometry and shaders
