This session introduces the mathematics of transformations and projections. Mastering 3D graphics and simulation requires a strong working knowledge of mathematics, particularly geometry, trigonometry, vectors and matrices. In this session we will apply these mathematical foundations to display and navigate a 3D world.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the basic mathematical concepts underlying computer graphics
  • Use these concepts in your programs
