This session introduces the mathematics of Newtonian mechanics. Just as with graphics, understanding the mathematics of how physical systems behave in the real world is essential to achieving high-fidelity results in games.

Learning Objectives:
  • Recall the definitions of key concepts such as position, velocity, acceleration, force, friction and restitution
  • Solve simple mathematical problems involving these key concepts
  • Write programs which feature realistic physics simulations
