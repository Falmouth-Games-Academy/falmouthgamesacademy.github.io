This session introduces mathematics for computer graphics. Mastering 3D graphics and simulation requires a strong working knowledge of mathematics, particularly geometry, trigonometry, vectors and matrices.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the basic mathematical concepts underlying computer graphics
  • Use these concepts in your programs
