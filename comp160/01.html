This session introduces the notion of software engineering quality and places emphasis on the importance of quality assurance processes in large projects. Though their value will not become apparent until you work on a large project, working knowledge of software engineering principles will be essential for your future work as a computing professionals. Beyond trivially small games, as the complexity of the games increases, the cost of fixing a single bug increases exponentially! So, this is a really important aspect of the course.


Studio Practice

Each week you will engage in mandatory studio practice under the supervision of mentors who will help you to develop your collaborative programming practice. You are also expected to attend a mandatory meeting with your product owner each week.
