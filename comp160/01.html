This session introduces the notion of software engineering quality and places emphasis on the importance of quality assurance processes in large projects. Though their value will not become apparent until you work on a large project, working knowledge of software engineering principles will be essential for your future work as a computing professionals on large-scale software development projects. Beyond trivially small games, as the complexity of the games increases, the cost of fixing a single bug increases exponentially!


Morning Session
