In this session, you conduct a 3-minute 'elevator pitch' to sell your own personal game concept to your peers. After which, a selection process based on multiple-transferable vote will be used to select the game designs to take forward and to allocate you to a team. You will be charged with developing a pre-production prototype of the chosen game.
