This session introduces core UML diagrams that you will use to describe the architecture of the software that you have designed. Further to this, it will also introduce the notion of refactoring and design patterns. Documenting source code and leveraging it to both, manage complexity, and, to avoid re-inventing solutions to well-known problems, will serve you well in the future.


Morning Session

Your supervised studio hours in the Morning Session are reserved for self-directed programming practice and work on your coursework projects. A mentor will be available to facilitate these tasks.


Afternoon Session
Learning Objectives:

  • Assess the efficiency of object interaction using a UML diagram
  • Apply knowledge of design patterns to refactor a game architecture
