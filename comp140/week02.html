This session begins to unpick HCI concepts such as inputs and outputs, as well as exploring the idea of interaction styles. This leads into a discussion around the importance of prototypes with a focus on how you might approach the assignment brief.

Learning Objectives:
  • Explain the role of input and output in system design
  • List and describe a variety of input and output devices, giving examples of situations where they may be appropriate
  • Explain what interaction styles are, while critically evaluating their respective advantages and disadvantages.
