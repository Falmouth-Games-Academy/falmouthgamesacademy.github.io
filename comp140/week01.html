This session introduces the brief and outlines the key principles and techniques of Human-Computer Interaction with an emphasis on usability and user-experience. As a group, you will research existing game controllers to help inform your approach to the brief.

Learning Objectives:
  • Describe the role of HCI in system design
  • Demonstrate an awareness of the HCI viewpoint which places the player at the centre of design.
  • Describe HCI's component disciplines and the contributions they make to it.
