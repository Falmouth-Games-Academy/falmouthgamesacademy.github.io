This session will introduce you to optimising the GPU, we will first look at the GPU Pipeline and then discuss various strategies for optimisation.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this session, you should be able to:

  • Recall the key stages of the graphics pipeline
  • Understand the GPU Debugger
  • Explain some of the key areas for optimisation
